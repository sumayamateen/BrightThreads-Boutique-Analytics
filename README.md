## Project Overview
End-to-end **retail analytics case study** applying the **OSEMN framework** to design strategies for achieving **10% sales growth** for BrightThreads Boutique.  
Demonstrates **data-driven decision-making**, predictive modeling, and business strategy execution.

**Client:** BrightThreads Boutique, New York  
**Objective:** Increase total sales by 10% and identify expansion opportunities  
**Timeline:** Year-long strategic initiative  

---

## Business Problems Solved
- **Strategic Growth Planning:** Data-driven roadmap to achieve **10% sales increase**
- **Customer Segmentation:** Identified **Value vs. High-Value Shoppers** with distinct behaviors
- **Marketing Efficiency:** Optimized social media ad spend using **ROI analysis**
- **Inventory Optimization:** Data-driven product selection and stock management
- **Data Quality Management:** Resolved duplicates, inconsistencies, and invalid pricing

---

## Key Results & Findings
- **Price Optimization:** Majority of orders cluster in **$75–$95 range**  
- **Customer Insights:** Two distinct segments with different buying behaviors  
- **Marketing ROI:** Positive correlation between ad spend and site visits  
- **Growth Confidence:** Predictive modeling confirms **10% sales increase achievable**  
- **Actionable Roadmap:** Clear 6-month implementation plan  

---

## OSEMN Framework Applied

### Obtain & Scrub
- Defined **SMART objectives** & KPIs  
- Collected and validated relevant data sources  
- Performed **data cleaning**: removed duplicates, fixed missing/inconsistent values  

### Explore & Model
- Conducted **EDA** to detect sales trends & customer behavior  
- Created **visual dashboards** for pattern recognition  
- Built **statistical models**: linear regression, clustering  
- Engineered features to improve model performance  
- Developed **predictive sales models**  

### Interpret & Present
- Translated analysis into **actionable business insights**  
- Prepared **stakeholder presentation decks**  
- Proposed **strategic growth recommendations** with a roadmap
